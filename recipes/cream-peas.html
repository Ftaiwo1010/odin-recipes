Cream peas recipe

Creamed peas make a great side dish.

Ingredients

  1. 2 cups frozen green peas, thawed
  2. ⅔ cup water
  3. ⅛ teaspoon salt
  4. 3 tablespoons butter
  5. ⅓ cup heavy cream
  6. 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  7. 1 tablespoon white sugar

Steps

  1. Combine peas, water, and salt in a medium saucepan. Bring to a boil, then stir in butter.
  2. Whisk together cream, flour, and sugar in a small bowl. Stir mixture into peas. Cook over medium-high heat until thick and bubbly, about 5 minutes.
