Garlic Spinach recipe

This garlic spinach has all the green flavor you love with tasty garlicky goodness.

Ingredients

  1. 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  2. 6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  3. 2 (10 ounce) bags fresh spinach
  4. ½ medium lemon, juiced
  5. ½ teaspoon garlic salt, or to taste

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a pot over medium heat. Add garlic; cook and stir until fragrant, about 2 minutes. Add spinach, a few handfuls at a time, stirring until wilted before adding more. Stir in lemon juice and season with garlic salt.
